THE WISE INVESTOR

YOUR LENDING CLUB® INVESTMENT STRATEGIST

Interested in investing in a loan on Lending Club® and wondering if it is a good business decision? This capstone project uses machine learning to help you predict whether a Lending Club® loan will get charged off, so you can make money and sit tight.

It is a risky game

Too Many bad loans

During 2009 ~ 2012, 1 in 7 on average loans gets charged off1. Out of the 421,095 loans issued in 2015, 9,241 of them charged off in less than 12 months. Among them, 4,565 were less than 20% paid, and 4,504 between 20~40%.

Be in charge

Once a loan gets listed on Lending Club®, it is up to the investors to judge if a loan is a good investment. We provide a machine learning approach to gauge the risks of a loan getting charged off. So invest in the good ones.

Why This Topic?

This project aims to discover and explore different facets of a business problem, and identify unexplored market.
